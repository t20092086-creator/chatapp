Privacy Policy

At RealTime Chat, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and ensure that your chats remain secure and private.

Information We Collect

RealTime Chat does not require account creation or personal data to use the service. We only process temporary chat messages to allow communication between users. We do not store chat history permanently on our servers.

How We Use Information

  • We only use data to deliver real-time chat functionality.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.
  • All communication is private and remains between chat participants.

Cookies

We may use basic cookies or local storage for session handling and improving user experience. These do not track your personal browsing habits.

Third-Party Ads

Our site may display ads provided by Google AdSense or other partners. These services may use cookies to deliver more relevant advertising. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

If you have privacy concerns, please contact us via the details on our Contact page.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data we may hold about you. Since we do not store personal information, this is generally not applicable. However, if you have specific concerns, please reach out to us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
